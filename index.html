Defy Aesthetic

Defy Aesthetic is a Maryland-based cosmetic boutique offering skincare, skin treatments and injectables. We worked with Defy Aesthetic to design their elevated and high-touch brand identity in addition to a custom e-commerce website, product packaging and in-store branding.

Brand Values

Brand Values Defy Aesthetic values providing a high-touch, unforgettable experience during treatments for each of their clients. When pulling imagery for the brand, we placed an emphasis on highlighting healthy, glowing skin.
